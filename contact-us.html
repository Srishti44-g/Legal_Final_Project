Frequently Asked Questions

After enrollment, you will receive access credentials to our secure learning platform within 24 hours. All course materials, including lecture recordings, case studies, and supplementary resources, will be available through your personalized dashboard. You can access these materials anytime during the course duration and for six months after completion.

Our courses are accredited by the American Bar Association (ABA) and the Continuing Legal Education (CLE) boards across all 50 states. Each course listing includes specific CLE credit information. For international recognition, we maintain partnerships with legal education authorities in the UK, Canada, Australia, and several EU countries. Detailed accreditation documentation is available upon request.

For specialized legal research assistance, you can submit a detailed request through our contact form by selecting "Legal Research Assistance" in the subject dropdown. Please provide specific information about your research area, key questions, jurisdictional requirements, and any deadlines. Our academic team will evaluate your request and connect you with a subject matter expert within 48-72 hours. For complex research projects, we may suggest scheduling a consultation call to better understand your needs.

We offer several partnership models for law firms, academic institutions, and legal organizations. These include co-branded continuing education programs, custom training solutions for firm associates, guest lecturer exchanges, research collaborations, and content licensing arrangements. For enterprise-level access to our platform, we provide institutional subscriptions with volume-based pricing. Select "Collaboration & Partnership" in the contact form to initiate a discussion with our partnerships team.

For immediate technical support, select "Technical Support" in our contact form and provide detailed information about the issue you're experiencing, including any error messages, the device and browser you're using, and steps to reproduce the problem. Our technical team aims to respond within 12-24 hours. For urgent matters during business hours, you can also use our live chat feature or call our technical support line directly at +1 (800) 555-0199.
