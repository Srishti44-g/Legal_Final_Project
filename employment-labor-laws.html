Employment & Labor Laws in India

Comprehensive guide to India's labor law framework covering wages, working conditions, social security, industrial disputes, and employment rights.


Key Employment & Labor Laws

The Code on Wages, 2019

Regulates minimum wages, equal pay, and timely payments for all workers.

The Industrial Relations Code, 2020

Governs strikes, trade unions, and employment termination procedures.

The Occupational Safety Code, 2020

Ensures workplace safety, health, and welfare standards.

The Social Security Code, 2020

Covers provident fund (PF), pension, and employee insurance benefits.
